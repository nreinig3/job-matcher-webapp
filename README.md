#### Problem:  
Traditional job search websites rely on keyword filters, which generate overwhelming yet imprecise results, forcing candidates to sift through many irrelevant postings while missing ideal opportunities disguised by different job titles. This inefficiency also causes problems for employers, who must contend with a high volume of misaligned applicants, making it difficult to find someone with the right fit.
